FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

We specialize in professional landscape design, installation, and related services including hardscaping (patios, retaining walls, walkways), softscaping (planting beds, trees, shrubs), irrigation systems, and ongoing maintenance. Whether you're after a full backyard transformation, curb appeal upgrades, or functional outdoor living spaces, we create custom designs tailored to your property, style, and needs.

Spring and early summer are ideal for most planting and installations, since milder weather helps new plants establish roots. Hardscaping — patios, walls, and similar projects — can often proceed through summer or fall, and fall is excellent for planting trees and shrubs. We recommend reaching out in winter or early spring to get on the schedule before peak season fills up.

Costs vary based on project scope, property size, materials, and design complexity. We provide detailed, no-obligation quotes after an initial consultation or site visit — factors like hardscaping, custom features, or plant selection all influence the final price. Our focus is on value-driven designs that enhance your property for years to come.

Landscaping is the overall process of designing and creating functional, beautiful outdoor spaces. Hardscaping refers to non-living elements like stone patios, retaining walls, pathways, and fire pits. Softscaping covers the living elements — plants, trees, lawns, and garden beds. We integrate both for balanced, sustainable results.

Yes. We offer end-to-end design-build services — starting with a consultation and custom design concepts, followed by detailed plans, material selection, and professional installation. This ensures your vision is executed seamlessly, with minimal stress on your end.

We're fully licensed and insured for all design, installation, and related work. We encourage clients to review our portfolio, client testimonials, and past projects — many of our clients come to us through referrals, and we prioritize clear communication, quality materials, and warranties on workmanship where applicable.

Timelines depend on project size and complexity. Smaller design consultations and plantings may take a few weeks, while full installations — including hardscaping — can range from 4 to 12 weeks or more. We provide realistic timelines during the proposal stage and keep you updated throughout.

New plants, lawns, and features need initial care — watering, mulching, and so on — to establish properly. We provide detailed care guides and can set up maintenance programs for pruning, weeding, seasonal cleanups, irrigation checks, and more. Proper ongoing care extends the life and beauty of your investment.
